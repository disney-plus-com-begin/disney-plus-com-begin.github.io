How to Sign Up for Disney Plus?


  • Connect to a reliable internet connection to turn on Disney Plus on the device you plan to make use of.
  • With your web browser enter this URL ‘disneyplus.com/begin’ and hit enter. You may also try ‘disneyplus.com/start’ if this address doesn’t work.
  • After you’ve logged on to the site Look for an opportunity to sign-up.
  • The next step is clicking on the “Sign-Up” button.
  • You’ll then be presented with the page that has a form. Fill in the form and fill in your name and email. Change your password and then type it into. Then click on the “Next” button to continue.
  • Then you will see an overview of the plans available as well as their fee for subscription.
  • Select a plan of actions.
  • For the final transaction to complete, you must enter your credit and billing details.
  • When the payment is finished and you are ready to close the transaction, click the “Finish” button to complete the transaction.
  • The subscription for Disney Plus has been completed. Clicking on “Start Watching now’, you can now access all your library of materials. Enjoy!

How to get an activation code for Disney Plus?


  • Download the Disney+ App: First, download the Disney+ app on your device. You can find it by searching for “Disney+” in your app store.
  • Log In: Open the Disney+ app and log in using your account credentials.
  • Retrieve the Code: Once logged in, an 8-digit code will appear on your screen. This code is your activation code.
  • Activate on the Website: On your computer or mobile device, visit Disneyplus.com/begin. Enter the 8-digit code there to verify your device.
  • Start Streaming: After successful activation, select Disneyplus.com/begin to enjoy Disney+ content on your device.

How to activate Disney Plus via Disneyplus.com/begin?


  • Launch the Disney+ app on your smart TV or streaming device.
  • Select the Log In option on the Disney+ app.
  • On your computer or mobile device, open a web browser and visit Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • You should see an 8-digit code displayed on your TV screen. Enter this code on the Disney Plus website.
  • Now Log in to your Disney+ account on the website using your email address and password.
  • Once you've successfully logged in, you'll see a confirmation message on your TV screen. You can now select Start Streaming to enjoy Disney+ content on your device.

FAQ's


I don't see the activation code on my TV screen

  • Check your TV settings: The activation code may be displayed on a hidden menu. You might need to check the "device info" or "about" section of your TV settings.
  • Relog into Disney Plus app: Sometimes, the code appears after you re-enter the app.
  • Contact Disney Plus Support: If you're still unable to locate the code, contact Disney Plus support for assistance.

My activation code is not working

  • Double check the code: Ensure you've entered the code correctly, including any special characters or numbers.
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable: A weak connection can hinder the activation process.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies: This can sometimes resolve activation issues.
  • Contact Disney Plus Support: If the problem persists, contact Disney Plus support for help.
